Hi! My name is Scott.


I'm a UX, Interaction, and Visual Designer. I'm interested in linking design, information, and technology to shape positive changes in the lives of people and the community.


I received my master's degree in Human-Computer Interaction at Carnegie Mellon University. I have working experience at several start-ups producing illustrations, digital print work, graphic web elements, and marketing deliverables. I previously received my Bachelor's degree in Interdisciplinary Computing and the Arts from the University of California, San Diego.


I enjoy playing with my dog, exploring new places, and (attempting to) get in touch with my spiritual side. I strive to better myself so I can help others and the amazing world around us.
