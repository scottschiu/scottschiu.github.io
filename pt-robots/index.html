Project Background

I am independently studying and working with recently elected CHI Academy member Jodi Forlizzi to develop iPad visuals for social and data interactions between mObi robots and physical therapy (PT) patients at the Vincentian Collaborative System.


This project began in January 2014 and will be completed in May 2014.
