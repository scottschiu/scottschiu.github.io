Project Background

The Master's program in Human-Computer Interaction (MHCI) Carnegie Mellon University allows students to work in teams with an industry sponsor (in my case, The Boeing Company) to implement an iterative research/development/design process.


This project began in January 2014 and will be completed in August 2014.


** Due to the proprietary nature of the project, I cannot disclose research findings online. However, I will document the process involved to achieve our final result.
