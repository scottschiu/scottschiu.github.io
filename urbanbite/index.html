Project Background

Urbanbite is a mobile app that allows urbanbites to purchase food prepared by cooks and for people to sell their own homemade food "Etsy style". The project was part of a course called Mobile Service Innovation, where we were asked to invent a new service that could compete with already existing apps.

Problem Statement

How do we make it easier for busy urbanites to eat healthy, delicious food?
